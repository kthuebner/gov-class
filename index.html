About the Course

Course Overview

What We Cover

In this course we will cover the ideas and key principles that shape our system of government. We will explore the Constitution, the Bill of Rights, the Branches of Government as well as landmark supreme court cases, current events and more.

Guiding Questions

  • What makes government legitimate?
  • How does the Constitution balance power and liberty?
  • How do the three branches check one another?
  • What rights do Americans actually have — and how are they protected?
  • How does politics shape policy and policy shape lives?
